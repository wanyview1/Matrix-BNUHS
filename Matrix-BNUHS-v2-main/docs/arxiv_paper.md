## Title

**Edu-Matrix: An AI-Powered Knowledge Salon Matrix System for Cross-Cultural Education**

---

## Abstract

We present Edu-Matrix, an innovative knowledge salon system designed for cross-cultural education. The system leverages AI to create immersive knowledge scenarios that bridge Eastern and Western educational paradigms. Our approach combines historical replication, knowledge capsules, and interactive scenarios to deliver personalized learning experiences. The system has been deployed and tested with 10+ knowledge salons, achieving an average DATM (Domain-Aware Theory Measure) score of 92.3.
